Why I started this

A Solution Born from Necessity

Living in temporary tin sheds shouldn't be the norm. I've designed EcoHaven to provide dignity, safety, and a sense of home.

Quick Assembly

Set up your home in hours, not weeks. Our innovative folding mechanism requires minimal tools and expertise.

Fully Modular

Start small and expand as needed. Add rooms, storage units, or connect multiple units together.

Weather Resistant

Built to withstand harsh conditions. Insulated walls keep you comfortable in all seasons.

Compact Transport

Collapses to 1/10th of its size. Easily fits in standard trucks for hassle-free relocation.

Reusable Design

Built for 100+ assembly cycles. Your investment travels with you from project to project.

Eco-Friendly

Made from sustainable materials with solar panel integration and rainwater harvesting options.
